About me

Greetings, fellow eco-enthusiasts and advocates of a cleaner future! I am the Litter Fairy, and I'm here to sprinkle a touch of magic on the city of Milwaukee. With a heart full of concern for our environment and a determination to tackle the trash problem head-on, I have dedicated myself to keeping our city and neighborhoods clean.

For as long as I can remember, I have been acutely aware of the impact our actions have on the world around us. Witnessing the staggering amount of waste polluting our streets and endangering our natural habitats, I knew I had to take action. Thus, the Litter Fairy was born—a persona embodying the essence of eco-consciousness and community empowerment.

Zones of Cleanliness

Welcome to my two magical zones of cleanliness: Murray Hill and the Lakefront! In Murray Hill, a vibrant neighborhood brimming with character, I wander through its charming streets, parks, and alleys, diligently collecting any litter that dares to mar its beauty. From the buzzing local shops to the peaceful residential areas, I ensure that every corner reflects the pride we hold for our community. And oh, the Lakefront! With its breathtaking views of Lake Michigan, I make it my mission to keep this natural treasure pristine. As I stroll along the sandy shores and explore the lush green spaces, I gather any remnants of carelessness left behind. Together, we can preserve the allure of Murray Hill and the serenity of the Lakefront, nurturing these remarkable spaces for everyone to enjoy, free from the clutches of litter.

52 Bags and counting...

Each bag represents countless hours spent meticulously picking up litter from the streets, parks, and neighborhoods I serve. It's a testament to the positive impact one dedicated individual can make in the fight against trash. As I continue on this journey, fueled by the desire to create a better future, I am grateful for the opportunity to make a tangible difference and inspire others to join in the effort. Together, we can transform our city, one bag at a time.
